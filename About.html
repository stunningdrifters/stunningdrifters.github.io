STUNNING DRIFTERS


Stunning Drifters is the team of M.I.E.T., MEERUT which manufactures electric Go-Kart. The biggest aim was to promote Go-Green and Digital INDIA given by Indian Government. By working on this project students learn to research, develop and manage various things not only related to project but also various real life experiences which transforms an individual into a professional, confident enough to knock the doors of industry that insures the future of country is best owed upon the shoulders of young professionals.


Specifications

  • Speed-65km/hr
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Smart Helmet
  • Smart Seat belt
  • Parking Assistant Sensors
  • Alcoholic Sensors
  • Emergency messages using GPRS Module
  • Self-Indicators
  • Battery Percentage Indicator
