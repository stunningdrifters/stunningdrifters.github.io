STUNNING DRIFTERS


Stunning Drifters is a team of Mechanical Department Of MIET Meerut who work on automobiles.


The Go-Kart

The Go-Kart was designed for the National Level EcoKart Championship in GBTU,Noida.The Kart has the Following Features:-

  1. Speed-65km/hr
  2. Bluetooth connectivity
  3. Smart Helmet
  4. Smart Seat belt
  5. Parking Assistant Sensors
  6. Alcoholic Sensors
  7. Emergency messages using GPRS Module
  8. Self-Indicators
  9. Battery Percentage Indicator
