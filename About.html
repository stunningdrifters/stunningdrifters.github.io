The Go-Kart

The Go-Kart was designed for the National Level EcoKart Championship in GBTU,Noida.The Kart has the Following Features:-

  1. Speed-35km/hr
  2. Bluetooth connectivity
  3. Smart Helmet
  4. Sensors For Parking
